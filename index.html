About this app

→
MoiHub is the ultimate campus companion for Moi University students. Our comprehensive platform seamlessly connects you with essential services including rental accommodations, local food delivery, a vibrant second-hand marketplace, campus e-shops, and much more. Designed specifically for the unique needs of campus life, MoiHub makes student living easier, more connected, and more affordable.
